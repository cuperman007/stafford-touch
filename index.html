Training

Training is on a Wednesday nights from 1900 until 2030 at Stafford Rugby Club

Everyone gets their first session free!

We have both social 'pay and play' pitches and pitch for those that want to work on drills, skills and play in local matches and events.

FAQs

How much does membership / training cost?

Membership of Stafford Rugby Club is encouraged and all registered players can play and train for free.

Otherwise, patron members pay £2 per session
and non-members pay £4 per session.

You can pay cash on the night to the organisers.

First session is free

I already play for another team, can I also play / train with you?

Yes, everyone is welcome.

What ages can train and play?

All players aged 12+ are welcome.

Juniors must have an responsible adult playing with them, or pitchside at all times. Juniors are reminded that these are adult sessions, and juniors are expected to concentrate and behave.

Do I need to be a registered ETA member?

No.
